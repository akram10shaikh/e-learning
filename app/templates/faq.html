About UPSKyED

What is UPSKyED

Udemy's mission is to create new possibilities for people and organizations everywhere. Our global marketplace features an extensive, multi-language library, which includes thousands of courses taught by real-world experts. You can take courses across a wide range of categories, some of which include: business & entrepreneurship, programming, academics, the arts, health & fitness, language, music, and more!

What do UPSKyED courses include?

The foundation of each UPSKyED course is its lectures, which can include videos, slides, and text. Each UPSKyED course is created, owned, and managed by the instructor(s). In addition, instructors can add resources and various types of practice activities, as a way to enhance the learning experience of students. !

Where can i access UPSKyED?

You can access your UPSKyED courses from various devices and platforms, on both PC and Mac desktops/laptops, as well as Android and iOS mobile devices.
